Your journey, step by step.

Connecting with a companion is safe, thorough, and simple. Here is how we ensure a seamless air travel experience from booking to baggage claim.

1

Share Your Flight Details

Enter your upcoming travel date, airline, flight number, and departure/arrival airports. Let us know exactly what kind of support you or your loved one needs.

2

Get Matched with a Companion

Our secure system scans for verified, highly-rated TravelBuddies booked on the exact same flight path. You'll receive profiles of potential matches to review.

3

Meet and Connect

Chat securely within our platform to get to know your TravelBuddy before the flight. Coordinate a clear, easy-to-find meeting point at the departure terminal layout.

4

Travel Safely Together

Your TravelBuddy assists with security checkpoints, guides you smoothly through airport gates, sits near or beside you on the plane, and helps navigate baggage arrival.
